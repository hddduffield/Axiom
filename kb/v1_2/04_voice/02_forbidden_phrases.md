# Forbidden Phrases & Red Flags

## NEVER USE — these phrases trigger plan rejection

### Generic AI-flavored hedge phrases
- "It's important to note that..."
- "Going forward..."
- "In today's complex tax landscape..."
- "Navigate the complexities..."
- "Holistic approach..."
- "Best practices..."
- "Cutting-edge strategies..."
- "Leverage opportunities..."
- "Synergies..."

### Marketing-speak
- "World-class..."
- "Bespoke..." (use "tailored" if needed)
- "Premier..."
- "Sophisticated investors like yourself..."
- "We pride ourselves on..."
- "Trusted partner..."

### Empty hedges
- "May or may not..."
- "Could potentially..."
- "It is worth considering whether..."
- "Generally speaking..."
- "From our perspective..."

### Credentialism
- "As your wealth advisor..."
- "We have determined that..."
- "It is our professional opinion that..." (state the opinion directly)

### Unfounded claims
- "Guaranteed savings"
- "Risk-free"
- "No downside"
- "Certain to..."

### Misrepresentation
- Anything that overstates PSA's role beyond financial planning + insurance
- Tax advice (use "your CPA recommends" or "tax-advised")
- Legal advice (use "your attorney" or "legally documented")
- Specific investment advice in plan body without IPS framing

## RED FLAG PHRASES — review for accuracy

### Phrases that may be inadvertently misleading
- "Tax-free" (often "tax-deferred" or "tax-free if X conditions met")
- "Permanent" (regulatory/statutory permanence subject to future legislation)
- "Always" / "Never" (rarely accurate in tax/estate context)
- "Best" / "Optimal" (claim that should be supported)

### Phrases that may overstate certainty
- "This will save you $X" (use "estimated annual benefit of $X")
- "After implementation, you will have..." (use "projected position post-implementation")
- "By next year, you will..." (use "the timeline targets...")

### Vague phrases requiring specificity
- "Regular review" → specify cadence (quarterly, annual)
- "Coordinate with your team" → name specific roles
- "When the time is right" → specify trigger or window

## VOICE CHECK FILTER

Before plan output, scan for:
- Forbidden phrases (any → reject; rewrite)
- Red flag phrases (any → review for accuracy; rewrite if uncertain)
- Numbers without source (any unsourced number → rewrite with source)
- Recommendations without quantification (any → add specific impact)
- Recommendations without role assignment (any → identify PSA / CPA / attorney / banker / etc.)
