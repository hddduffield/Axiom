# Tone Toward Partners (CPA, Attorney, Banker, Insurance, etc.)

## CORE STANCE

PSA Wealth plays "quarterback." We coordinate. We do NOT dictate to other professionals.

When the plan references CPA, attorney, banker, insurance broker, or other specialist:
- Acknowledge their role and authority
- Defer to their judgment in their area
- Position PSA as coordinator, not authority

## SENTENCE PATTERNS

### CPA references
✅ "Your CPA confirms the math; PSA tracks the timing."
✅ "We coordinate quarterly with your CPA on estimated payments."
✅ "The §409A compliance analysis is your CPA's call; we provide the structural design."

❌ "We instruct your CPA to file..."
❌ "Tell your accountant to..."
❌ "The CPA needs to..."

### Attorney references
✅ "Your attorney drafts the trust document; PSA coordinates the funding."
✅ "Specialist counsel on captives is mandatory; we'd refer to [firm]."
✅ "We work with your attorney on annual operating agreement audit."

❌ "Have your attorney..."
❌ "Get your lawyer to..."
❌ "Tell counsel..."

### Banker references
✅ "Your sell-side banker manages the competitive process; PSA prepares you for the diligence."
✅ "We don't run M&A processes; we coordinate with the banker you select."

❌ "We will direct the banker..."
❌ "PSA negotiates the transaction..."

### Insurance broker references (P&C, health)
✅ "Your P&C broker places the umbrella; we review the result."
✅ "PSA doesn't place P&C; coordinates with your P&C team."

❌ "We will arrange your homeowner coverage..."
❌ "PSA handles all your insurance needs..." (overstates)

## CRITICAL NUANCE

PSA does sell life insurance through MML / MassMutual relationship. So:

✅ "PSA places life insurance through our MML / MassMutual relationship; coordinates with your CPA on tax structure and your attorney on ownership."
✅ "We handle the life insurance underwriting; your attorney drafts the ILIT; your CPA reports the structure correctly."

In plan body, when life insurance is involved, the placement role is implicit — don't over-claim, don't under-claim. The plan output reflects that PSA is involved in placement; the CPA/attorney are handling tax and document drafting.

## DEALING WITH PARTNER MISTAKES OR DELAYS

If a CPA missed a filing or attorney is behind on drafting:
- Don't blame in writing
- Don't gossip across plans
- Note operationally that the dependency exists; coordinate to address

In plan or memo language:
✅ "Coordinating with [CPA] on ${item}."
✅ "Awaiting [Attorney]'s draft of ${document}."
✅ "Plan dependency: ${item} from [CPA]."

❌ "Your CPA didn't file..."
❌ "The attorney is dragging on..."
❌ "We've been waiting weeks for..."

## SHARED DOCUMENTS

When PSA shares a plan with the CPA or attorney directly:
- Tone: peer-to-peer professional
- Acknowledge their authority; don't second-guess their work product
- "Welcome your input on the structuring before we finalize with the client"
- "Defer to your judgment on §409A compliance"
