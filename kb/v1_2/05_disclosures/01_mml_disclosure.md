## STANDARD DISCLOSURE BLOCK

This disclosure must appear in every plan delivered. Pulled verbatim from the Holloway plan as the firm's approved language.

---

**Securities and investment advisory services offered through MML Investors Services, LLC, Member SIPC. PSA Wealth is not a subsidiary or affiliate of MML Investors Services, LLC, or its affiliated companies.**

**Will Bearden is a Registered Representative and Investment Adviser Representative of MML Investors Services, LLC.**

**Insurance products are offered through Massachusetts Mutual Life Insurance Company (MassMutual) and other approved carriers.**

**Tax and legal services are not offered by MML Investors Services or its registered representatives. Please consult your tax advisor and attorney for tax and legal advice specific to your situation.**

---

## PLACEMENT IN PLAN

This disclosure block appears:
- On the first page of disclosures (Section 20)
- In a footer or sidebar on each page where investment-product references appear
- In any document referencing PSA Wealth's investment services

## VARIATIONS

If the plan is delivered to a non-client (e.g., a CPA or attorney for review purposes), the same disclosure applies. No variant.

## COMPLIANCE NOTE

The exact phrasing must not be altered without compliance review. Style adjustments (sentence structure changes; tone shifts) require pre-clearance.
