# Estimates and Projections Disclaimer

## STANDARD DISCLAIMER

For any plan containing numerical projections (essentially all plans):

---

**ESTIMATES AND PROJECTIONS DISCLAIMER**

Numerical projections in this plan are estimates based on stated assumptions and current law. Actual outcomes will differ from projections.

Specifically:
- Investment return projections assume a hypothetical compound annual return rate. Actual returns will vary based on market conditions, asset selection, and timing.
- Tax savings calculations assume current federal and state tax rates and limits. Future legislation may alter these.
- Estate transfer projections assume current §7520 and AFR rates as published by the IRS for the relevant month. These rates change monthly.
- Insurance premium estimates are based on representative pricing; actual premiums depend on underwriting outcomes.
- Business valuation references are illustrative and assume the cited valuation methodology; market valuation at any actual transaction depends on market conditions, buyer interest, and process.
- Projections of compounded value over multi-decade horizons are directional, not predictive.

This plan should be reviewed annually and updated as facts change, assumptions update, or legislation evolves.
