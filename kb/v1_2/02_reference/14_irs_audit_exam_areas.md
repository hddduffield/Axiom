# IRS Audit / Exam Priority Areas — Reference

Awareness of current IRS enforcement priorities helps the firm identify which planning strategies require extra documentation rigor and which to avoid (landmines).

## CURRENT DIRTY DOZEN (PERSISTENT) — IRS PUBLIC PRIORITY LIST

1. **Syndicated conservation easements** — addressed in REC-CHR-011 (LANDMINE)
2. **Micro-captive insurance arrangements** — addressed in REC-RSK-016 (LANDMINE)
3. **Abusive offshore arrangements**
4. **Improper claims for ERTC / R&D credits** — affects REC-TAX-009 if vendor not reputable
5. **Inflated charitable deductions** — affects appraisal discipline
6. **Improperly claimed business credits**
7. **Promoter-driven schemes** — categorical avoid

## OBBBA-SPECIFIC ENFORCEMENT FOCUS

OBBBA expanded IRS funding for high-income enforcement. Likely audit priority:
- High-income individuals ($1M+ income)
- Complex partnerships
- Large estates (Form 706)
- §199A QBI claims aggressive interpretations
- §1202 QSBS qualifications (especially aggressive structuring)
- Bonus depreciation cost segregation aggressive applications

## ESTATE / GIFT EXAM PATTERNS

- **Form 709 inadequate disclosure:** can extend statute of limitations indefinitely
- **Discount aggressive without qualified appraisal:** routinely challenged
- **§2036 attack on FLPs:** death-bed FLP funding routinely attacked (Estate of Powell, Strangi)
- **Crummey power formality lapses:** notice procedure documentation reviewed
- **Defective grantor trust structures:** §675 mechanism vs §2036 inclusion

## INCOME TAX EXAM PATTERNS

- **S-Corp reasonable compensation** (REC-TAX-002): too-low W-2 with high distributions challenged
- **Augusta Rule (§280A(g))** (REC-TAX-005): need real meeting, real fee, documentation
- **Family-employed children** (REC-TAX-004, REC-FAM-003): real work; reasonable wages
- **Cost segregation aggressive splits**: building components reclassified
- **Real estate professional status** (§469): hours and material participation documented
- **§199A SSTB classification**: facts-and-circumstances disputes

## DOCUMENTATION DISCIPLINE — PSA HOUSE STANDARDS

For any strategy in this knowledge base:
- Real economic substance, not just paper structure
- Contemporaneous documentation (not after-the-fact)
- Qualified appraisals where valuation matters
- Independent counsel engagement
- Defensible position with citation of supporting authority
