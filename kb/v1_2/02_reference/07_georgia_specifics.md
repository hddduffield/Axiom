# Georgia-Specific Tax & Estate Reference

## INCOME TAX

- **2025/2026 rate:** 5.39% → **5.19%** for tax years beginning 1/1/2025 (HB 1015)
- **Schedule:** drops 0.10% per year going forward toward **4.99%** floor
- **Filing:** Form 500 personal; Form 600 corporate; Form 600S S-Corp; Form 700 partnership

## PASS-THROUGH ENTITY TAX (PTET) — HB 149

- **Rate:** matches individual income tax rate (5.19% for 2025)
- **Election:** annual; checkbox on Form 600S Schedule 1 or Form 700 Schedule 3
- **Eligibility:** S-Corps, partnerships, LLCs taxed as either; **single-member LLCs not eligible**
- **Estimated payments:** required at entity level via Form 602-ES; **not from personal funds**
- **Owner credit:** non-refundable on Form 500
- See REC-TAX-001

## ESTATE / INHERITANCE TAX

**Georgia has no state estate or inheritance tax.** Federal estate tax only.

## GEORGIA STATE 529 PLAN (Path2College 529)

- **State deduction:** up to $4,000 per beneficiary (single) / $8,000 (MFJ) for residents
- **Front-load:** state deduction does NOT scale with federal 5-year averaging — state deduction is annual

## REAL ESTATE / TRANSFER TAX

- **Real estate transfer tax:** $1.00 per $1,000 of consideration; modest

## TRUST INCOME TAXATION

Georgia taxes trust income if:
- Trust created by GA resident, OR
- Trust administered in Georgia (resident trustee, resident administration), OR
- Trust has GA-source income

**Planning:** trusts properly sited and administered in DE/SD/NV/WY/AK with no GA nexus avoid GA trust income tax.
