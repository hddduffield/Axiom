# §1202 QSBS — Reference (Post-OBBBA)

## OBBBA CHANGES (Stock issued AFTER July 4, 2025)

| Item | Pre-OBBBA | Post-OBBBA |
|---|---|---|
| Holding period for 100% exclusion | 5+ years | 5+ years (unchanged) |
| New: 50% exclusion | n/a | 3-4 years |
| New: 75% exclusion | n/a | 4-5 years |
| Per-issuer cap | $10M | **$15M** (or 10× basis) |
| Aggregate gross asset cap | $50M | **$75M** |
| Inflation indexing of cap | none | beginning 2027 |

**Stock issued ON or BEFORE July 4, 2025:** original rules apply (5-year hold for 100%; $10M cap; $50M asset test).

## TIERED EXCLUSION (POST-OBBBA)

| Holding Period | Exclusion |
|---|---|
| 3+ years | 50% |
| 4+ years | 75% |
| 5+ years | 100% |

**Unexcluded gain (50% remaining at 3 yrs; 25% at 4 yrs):** taxed at **28%** plus NIIT — NOT 15%/20%.

## QUALIFICATION REQUIREMENTS (UNCHANGED)

Stock must be:
1. **Domestic C-Corporation** — S-Corp, LLC, partnership stock NOT QSBS
2. **Original issuance** — purchased from corporation (not secondary market) for cash, services, or property (NOT for other stock except in tax-free reorganization)
3. **Qualified small business** — aggregate gross assets ≤ $75M (post-OBBBA) immediately before and after issuance
4. **Active business** — at least 80% of assets used in qualified trade or business for substantially all of holding period
5. **Not SSTB** — services like health, law, accounting, financial services excluded; some manufacturing/tech/retail businesses qualify

## §1045 ROLLOVER (UNCHANGED)

Sale of QSBS held >6 months can be rolled into new QSBS within 60 days. Holding periods tack. Useful when investor sells before 5-year hold.

## STATE CONFORMITY

- **Conforming (federal exclusion respected):** majority of states
- **Non-conforming:** Alabama, California, Mississippi, New Jersey, Pennsylvania
- **Partial:** Hawaii, Massachusetts
- **Georgia:** [VERIFY 2026 — confirm current GA conformity status]

**Planning:** non-grantor trust in no-tax state (DE, NV, SD, WY, AK) can hold QSBS to avoid both federal (via §1202) and state tax on gain.

## §1202 MULTIPLICATION VIA GIFTING

Per-taxpayer cap is per-issuer. Gifts to family members or non-grantor trusts pre-sale "multiply" the available exclusion. Each new holder has independent $15M cap. See REC-SPC-010.
