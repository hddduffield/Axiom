# Master Sequence — Post-Exit Engagement

For clients who have completed (or are immediately post-) a liquidity event.

## IMMEDIATE POST-CLOSE (T+0 to T+90 days)

### Wave 1 — Stabilization
1. Verify all transaction documents executed and proceeds correctly received
2. **REC-INV-002** — Personal cash layering for transaction proceeds
3. **REC-INV-001** — Business cash if continuing business activity
4. **REC-INV-009** — Treasury Direct holdings as initial deployment
5. **REC-RET-009** — Defined-benefit plan termination (if applicable; coordinated with sale)
6. Confirm all stay bonus and SERP payouts proceeding per agreement

## YEAR T+0 to T+1 (Tax-Year Optimization)

### Wave 2 — Tax Planning for Transaction Year
7. **REC-INV-008** — Roth conversion bracket modeling for current and future gap years
8. **REC-CHR-001** — DAF funding (if not already funded pre-LOI)
9. **REC-INV-007** — Tax-loss harvesting on existing positions
10. Confirm §1202 exclusion claimed correctly (REC-TAX-008, REC-SPC-010)
11. **REC-TAX-015** — QOZ investment (if eligible deferral and time-allowed)

## YEAR T+1 (Portfolio Construction)

### Wave 3 — Portfolio Building
12. **REC-INV-006** — Post-transaction concentration unwind (if rollover equity / restricted stock / earnout)
13. Investment Policy Statement: define target allocation
14. **REC-INV-003** — Direct indexing in taxable
15. **REC-INV-004** — Asset location optimization
16. **REC-INV-007** — Ongoing tax-loss harvesting integration
17. **REC-INV-010** — Municipal bond allocation
18. **REC-INV-011** — Private markets allocation framework (paced over multiple vintages)
19. **REC-RSK-013** — PPLI evaluation if scale appropriate

## YEAR T+1 to T+3 (Stabilization to Family Office Mode)

### Wave 4 — Family Wealth Architecture
20. Re-evaluate REC-EST-009 (SLAT) — fund or expand if not yet done
21. Re-evaluate REC-EST-006/007 (GRAT) — continue laddered cadence
22. Re-evaluate REC-EST-008 (IDGT) — if any non-voting interest remained or other appreciating asset available
23. **REC-EST-014** — Portability election (Form 706, if first spouse passes; 5-year window via Rev. Proc. 2022-32 if late)
24. **REC-EST-016** — Decanting old/stale trusts if needed
25. **REC-EST-020** — Beneficiary audit on all retirement, insurance, account designations
26. **REC-FAM-006/007/008** — Family governance (mission, meetings, education)

## YEAR T+1+ (Charitable & Specialty)

### Wave 5 — Charitable Sophistication
27. **REC-CHR-007** — Private foundation (if scale and engagement)
28. **REC-CHR-008** — DAF-to-foundation transition (if foundation established)
29. **REC-CHR-013** — QCD from IRA at age 70½+
30. **REC-CHR-009 / REC-CHR-010 / REC-CHR-012** — Bargain sale, PIF, CGA (specific cases)

### Wave 6 — Specialty
31. **REC-SPC-001** — Multi-state trust situs evaluation
32. **REC-SPC-002** — NING / DING (rare for Georgia)
33. **REC-SPC-003** — Crypto planning (if applicable)
34. **REC-SPC-005** — Concentrated stock hedging (if rollover or restricted stock)
35. **REC-SPC-006** — Structured charitable bailout (CRUT + ILIT) — if both charitable and family preservation
36. **REC-SPC-007** — Reverse mortgage evaluation (rare; specific senior cases)

## ONGOING ANNUAL CADENCE

- Q1: estate / gift / GST review; trust funding decisions; volatile rates check
- Q2: insurance audit; P&C review (REC-RSK-008/009/010); family meeting
- Q3: charitable strategy; DAF/foundation; QCD planning if 70½+
- Q4: tax projection; loss harvesting; bracket fill / Roth conversion; §409A elections for next year
- Year-end: gifts; Crummey notices; final plan review

## COORDINATION WITH AGING / TRANSITION

As client ages:
- LTC (REC-RSK-011/012) implementation while still insurable
- REC-FAM-005 aging-parent coordination
- POA / healthcare directive refresh
- Gradual delegation of decision-making to next-gen via family governance
- Ultimate transition planning (gifting acceleration if exemption sunsetting concerns return)
