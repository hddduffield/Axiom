## WHAT IT IS

Annual review of all entity operating documents:
- Operating agreement (LLC)
- Shareholder agreement (S-Corp)
- Buy/sell agreement
- Inter-entity agreements (lease, license, services)
- Any amendments since last review

---

## WHY WE RECOMMEND IT

Documents drift out of sync with reality. Owners change, valuations change, business circumstances change. Annual audit prevents the most common transaction-time discovery: documents that don't match operating practice.

---

## QUANTIFIED IMPACT FRAMEWORK
- Cost: $1K-$3K annual review
- Avoided transaction friction: $10K-$50K+ in deal-time amendments

---

## IMPLEMENTATION STEPS

1. Annual review with attorney
2. Identify drift between documents and practice
3. Amendments as needed
4. Owner sign-off on amendments
