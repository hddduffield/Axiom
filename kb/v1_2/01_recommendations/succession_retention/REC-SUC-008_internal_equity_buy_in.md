# [REC-SUC-008] — Internal Equity Buy-In Path for Operating Partner

## METADATA
- **ID:** REC-SUC-008
- **Status:** Active
- **Category:** Succession & Retention / Equity Path
- **Engagement archetypes:** Active-No-Exit, Pre-Exit (when key partner stays)
- **Plan section placement:** "Recommendations — Business" → "Equity Path"
- **Last verified:** April 2026

## TRIGGERING CONDITIONS

### Structured logic
```
TRIGGER if ALL of:
  - Key non-owner ready to take partner equity (the firm's "operating partner" archetype)
  - Owner willing to dilute meaningfully or transition gradually
  - Consideration available (cash, financing, sweat-equity earn-in)
```

### Natural-language explanation
A defined path for a non-owner key employee to acquire equity over time. Multiple structures possible: outright purchase at FMV with seller financing; vesting-based earn-in; profits-interest-to-equity conversion; performance-driven dilution.

### Hard disqualifiers
- No identified candidate
- Mutual unwillingness

## WHAT IT IS
Document and structure a multi-year path for an operating partner to become a meaningful equity holder:
- Triggering valuation (current + pre-defined formula or appraisal)
- Acquisition mechanism (purchase, vesting, profits-interest conversion)
- Payment terms (cash, seller financing at AFR, performance bonuses applied to purchase)
- Restrictions (transfer restrictions, drag/tag rights, buy-back triggers)

## WHY WE RECOMMEND IT
For owner who wants to slow-transition rather than full-sale, internal equity path keeps the business in "family" while compensating the operator. Gives owner partial liquidity over time.

## VARIATIONS
- **Outright purchase + seller financing:** operator buys equity at appraised value; owner finances over 5-10 years at AFR
- **Vesting earn-in:** equity vests over service period
- **Profits-interest-to-equity conversion:** start with profits interest (REC-SUC-007); convert to capital interest at performance milestones
- **Combined with stay bonus + bonus-applied-to-purchase:** annual bonus applied to equity purchase

## QUANTIFIED IMPACT FRAMEWORK

### Components
- Owner partial liquidity from operator's purchase
- Operator's ownership stake at vesting
- Tax treatment for both
- Long-term path to operator becoming primary owner (alternative to outside sale)

## IMPLEMENTATION STEPS
1. Define target ownership (e.g., operator at 25-40% over 5-10 years)
2. Determine valuation methodology
3. Structure purchase mechanism
4. Operating agreement updates
5. Buy/sell arrangements between owner and operator
6. Annual valuation update; periodic equity transfer events
