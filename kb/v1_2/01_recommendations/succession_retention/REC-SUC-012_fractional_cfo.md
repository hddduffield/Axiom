TRIGGER if ALL of:
  - FR.has_business == True

### Natural-language explanation
A fractional CFO provides senior finance leadership at part-time cost. For owner-led businesses pre-transaction, this fills the gap between an owner-operator's bookkeeper-controller and the financial sophistication a buyer expects in diligence — clean reporting, monthly close discipline, working-capital management, board-grade financials.

### Hard disqualifiers
- Existing finance leadership adequate
- Business too small

## WHAT IT IS
Engagement of senior CFO-level talent at fractional intensity (typically 8-20 hours/week). Responsibilities:
- Monthly close discipline
- Board-grade financial reporting
- Working capital management
- Banking relationships
- Pre-transaction diligence preparation
- Coordination with QofE firm and banker

## WHY WE RECOMMEND IT
Buyers discount businesses with weak finance function. Fractional CFO closes the gap pre-transaction; cost typically $80K-$200K/year vs. full-time CFO at $250K+. Often pays for itself many times over through cleaner diligence and higher transaction valuation.

## QUANTIFIED IMPACT FRAMEWORK

### Components
- Cleaner financials → smoother diligence
- Working capital optimization (sometimes 5-10% transaction value impact)
- Banker/QofE coordination
- Avoidance of re-trade events from accounting surprises

## IMPLEMENTATION STEPS
1. Identify fractional CFO firm or individual (industry experience preferred)
2. Define scope: monthly close, reporting, transaction prep
3. Engagement letter
4. Onboard with team (controller, bookkeeper, banker)
5. Monthly cadence; quarterly board-grade financials
