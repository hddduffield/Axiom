### Natural-language explanation
Smooth income across years to fill lower brackets and avoid spikes into higher brackets. Coordinate IRA distributions, deferred comp triggers, capital gain realization, and charitable timing.

---

## WHAT IT IS

Multi-year tax-smoothing strategy: deliberately accelerate or defer recognized income to fill the bottom of high tax brackets without bumping into higher ones. Coordinated across all income types.

---

## WHY WE RECOMMEND IT

Post-exit clients often have lumpy income (large deferred comp triggers, irregular IRA distributions, episodic capital gains). Smoothing reduces lifetime tax bill vs. concentrated recognition.

---

## QUANTIFIED IMPACT FRAMEWORK

Highly fact-specific. Typical: 2-3% reduction in average effective tax rate over 10-year window via deliberate smoothing.

---

## IMPLEMENTATION STEPS

1. Multi-year income projection.
2. Identify bracket-fill opportunities each year.
3. Coordinate distributions, conversions, gain realization.
4. Annual review.
