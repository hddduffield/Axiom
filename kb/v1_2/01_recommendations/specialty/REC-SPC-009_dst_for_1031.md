### Natural-language explanation
A DST is a fractional-interest ownership in commercial real estate qualifying as like-kind for §1031. Used when direct replacement isn't available within 45-day window. Trade-off: passive ownership; sponsor-managed; lower returns; resale challenges.

### Hard disqualifiers
- Direct replacement available
- Owner wants control

## WHAT IT IS
Trust under Delaware law holding commercial real estate; investors purchase fractional interests. Beneficial owners under §1031. Sponsor manages; investors passive. Hold periods 5-10 years until sponsor exit.

## WHY WE RECOMMEND IT (when triggered)
Identification flexibility; institutional-quality assets via reputable sponsors.

## QUANTIFIED IMPACT FRAMEWORK
- Like-kind treatment for 1031
- Fractional ownership commercial property
- Sponsor fees (high)
- Distributions and eventual sale

## IMPLEMENTATION STEPS
1. Identify reputable sponsor (track record matters)
2. Subscription documents
3. Closing within 1031 timeline
4. Hold through sponsor exit
5. Reinvest at exit
