# [REC-SPC-007] — Reverse Mortgage for Senior Liquidity

## METADATA
- **ID:** REC-SPC-007
- **Status:** Active-Cautioned
- **Category:** Specialty
- **Engagement archetypes:** Aging clients with home equity
- **Plan section placement:** "Senior Liquidity"
- **Last verified:** April 2026

## TRIGGERING CONDITIONS

### Structured logic
```
TRIGGER if ALL of:
  - Owner age 62+
  - Substantial home equity
  - Liquidity need or cash-flow gap
  - Plans to remain in home
```

### Natural-language explanation
Reverse mortgages (HECM) allow homeowners 62+ to draw against home equity without monthly payments; loan accrues and is repaid at sale or death. Useful for specific senior cash-flow situations; high fees and accrual; not appropriate for many.

### Hard disqualifiers
- Plans to move in <5 years
- Heirs object on family-asset grounds
- Insufficient equity

## WHAT IT IS
HECM loan secured by home; no monthly payments; balance grows; due when borrower no longer occupies home. Borrower retains title.

## WHY WE RECOMMEND IT (selectively)
Specific senior cash-flow situations. Generally not first choice; explore alternatives first (downsize, HELOC, family arrangement).

## QUANTIFIED IMPACT FRAMEWORK
- Loan amount available (age, home value, rate dependent)
- Compounding interest
- Closing costs (2-5%)
- Eventual equity reduction

## IMPLEMENTATION STEPS
1. HUD-required counseling
2. FHA-approved lender
3. Underwriting and appraisal
4. Closing
5. Draw schedule

## SEQUENCING DEPENDENCIES
- CONSIDER ALTERNATIVES FIRST

## COMMON MISTAKES
- Over-borrowing
- Spouse not on loan
- Property tax / insurance / maintenance failures (default trigger)

## CLIENT CONVERSATION FRAMING
> "Reverse mortgage is a specific tool for specific situations. We typically explore alternatives first — downsizing, HELOC, family arrangements. If those don't fit, HECM provides liquidity without monthly payments. High closing costs and accrual are real."

## CAVEATS & DISQUALIFIERS
- Closing costs high
- Accrual reduces equity
- Default risks (taxes, insurance, maintenance)
