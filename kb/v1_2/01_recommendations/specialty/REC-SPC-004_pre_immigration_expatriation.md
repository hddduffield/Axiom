## TRIGGERING CONDITIONS

TRIGGER if ANY of:

  - Pre-expatriation by US citizen/LPR

### Natural-language explanation
Cross-border tax planning is highly specialized. Pre-immigration: structures established before US tax residency preserve foreign income/assets from US taxation. Pre-expatriation: §877A "exit tax" applies to covered expatriates; planning before triggering events.

### Hard disqualifiers
- No cross-border movement

## WHAT IT IS
Highly technical multi-jurisdictional planning. Beyond PSA's typical scope; requires specialist international tax counsel.

## WHY WE RECOMMEND IT
Cross-border events trigger massive consequences without planning. Most must be done BEFORE the triggering event.

3. Structure pre-event
4. Foreign counsel coordination
