## TRIGGERING CONDITIONS

### Structured logic
```
TRIGGER if ALL of:
  - Crypto holdings > $100K
  - No estate plan addressing access
  - Tax optimization not in place

DISQUALIFY if:
  - Already addressed
```

### Natural-language explanation
Crypto introduces specific issues: access (private keys, exchanges) on death; basis tracking (every transaction taxable); state tax sourcing; charitable contribution mechanics; LTCG hold strategies.

### Hard disqualifiers
- Already addressed
- Immaterial holding

## WHAT IT IS
Comprehensive treatment:
- Custody and access (private keys, hardware wallets, exchange credentials)
- Basis tracking (CoinTracker, Koinly)
- Estate documents addressing digital assets
- Tax planning (>1 year for LTCG; FMV deduction on appreciated crypto via DAF)
- RUFADAA fiduciary access

## WHY WE RECOMMEND IT
Material crypto without planning creates execution problems and tax risk.

## IMPLEMENTATION STEPS
1. Inventory holdings
2. Estate documents updated for digital assets
3. Executor access plan (secure but accessible)
4. Tax reporting via specialty software
5. Charitable strategy via DAF if applicable
6. Annual review
