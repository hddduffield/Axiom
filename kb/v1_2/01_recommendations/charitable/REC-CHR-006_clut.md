## TRIGGERING CONDITIONS
Same as CLAT but with unitrust payment (% of trust value, recomputed annually) rather than fixed annuity.

### Natural-language explanation
CLUT is the unitrust variant of CLAT. Pays charity a % of trust value annually for term; family takes remainder at end. Less common than CLAT in practice; mathematically different §7520 sensitivity.

## WHAT IT IS
Trust pays charity unitrust amount (%, typically 5-7%) of annual trust value for term; remainder to family at end.

## WHY WE RECOMMEND IT (rare)
When asset valuation expected to grow substantially, CLUT's annual revaluation captures more value into charity over time, leaving smaller remainder. Generally CLAT preferred for family-remainder maximization.
