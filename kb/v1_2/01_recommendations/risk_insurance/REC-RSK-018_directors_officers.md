# [REC-RSK-018] — D&O Coverage for Outside Board Service

## METADATA
- **ID:** REC-RSK-018
- **Status:** Active
- **Category:** Risk & Insurance
- **Engagement archetypes:** When client serves on outside boards
- **Plan section placement:** "Recommendations — Personal Risk"
- **Last verified:** April 2026

## TRIGGERING CONDITIONS

### Structured logic
```
TRIGGER if ALL of:
  - Client serves on board of directors of any entity (own business; outside companies; nonprofits; community organizations)
  - Outside organizations may not have adequate D&O coverage
  - Personal exposure exists for board decisions

DISQUALIFY if:
  - No board service
  - All boards have verified adequate coverage
```

### Natural-language explanation
Directors and officers of corporations and nonprofits face personal liability for board decisions. Most organizations carry D&O coverage, but limits may be inadequate for HNW directors who become litigation targets. Personal D&O ("Side A DIC" coverage or personal umbrella with D&O coverage) protects when entity coverage is insufficient or unavailable.

### Hard disqualifiers
- No board service
- Adequate entity-level coverage verified

## WHAT IT IS
Personal D&O coverage that responds when:
- Entity D&O coverage is exhausted, denied, or insufficient
- Entity is insolvent and cannot indemnify
- Specific exclusions apply

## WHY WE RECOMMEND IT
Outside board members are increasingly named in litigation. Entity D&O has policy limits, exclusions, and may not respond if entity is insolvent. For HNW directors, personal D&O fills the gap.

## QUANTIFIED IMPACT FRAMEWORK
- Coverage when entity D&O is exhausted
- Coverage when entity is insolvent
- Defense cost coverage

## IMPLEMENTATION STEPS
1. Inventory board service positions
2. Request entity D&O policy summary from each board's secretary
3. Identify gaps in coverage
4. Add personal D&O endorsement (often available through HNW carriers like Chubb, AIG, Pure)
5. Annual review

## CAVEATS & DISQUALIFIERS
- Some policies exclude regulated-industry boards
- Personal D&O typically excludes intentional acts, fraud
